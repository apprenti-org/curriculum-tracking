Syllabus: AI Foundations

Operations Support Specialist — Network 20 hours DRAFT

Course Description

AI Foundations provides a comprehensive introduction to artificial intelligence and machine learning for operations professionals. This course covers AI fundamentals, generative AI technologies (including large language models and co-pilots), data organization, and responsible AI implementation. Learners will gain practical experience with modern AI tools and develop an understanding of how AI can enhance operational workflows and decision-making. The course emphasizes ethical considerations, data quality, and real-world applications across various industry contexts.

Summative Assessment: AI Project Implementation

Learners will complete a capstone project that involves either analyzing a business problem suitable for AI intervention, designing an AI-driven solution, or implementing a practical use case with available AI tools. The assessment evaluates understanding of AI concepts, ability to organize and prepare data, ethical reasoning about AI deployment, and proficiency with selected AI platforms. Learners will present findings and recommendations, demonstrating how AI can address operational challenges responsibly.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Define artificial intelligence, machine learning, and deep learning, and distinguish between different AI paradigms
  2. Explain how large language models work and design effective prompts for AI assistants
  3. Identify data quality issues and apply preparation techniques for AI model training
  4. Evaluate AI tools and platforms for operational use cases
  5. Analyze ethical implications of AI deployment and apply responsible AI frameworks
  6. Implement practical AI solutions to address operational challenges
  7. Assess the business value and ROI of AI-driven initiatives

Course Outline

  • AI vs. ML vs. deep learning definitions
Lesson: Machine Learning Basics

  • Dataset lifecycle
Lesson: Real-World Applications
  • Industry use cases and business value

Lesson: LLMs and Generative AI

Lesson: Prompt Engineering
