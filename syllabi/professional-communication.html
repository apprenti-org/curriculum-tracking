Syllabus: Professional Communication

Operations Support Specialist — Network 4 hours DRAFT

Course Description

Professional Communication develops essential soft skills for IT operations professionals working in collaborative technical environments. This course covers effective written and verbal communication, working with diverse stakeholders, professional conduct, and ethical decision-making. Learners will develop the interpersonal competencies needed for successful careers in IT operations, including teamwork, conflict resolution, and customer service excellence.

Summative Assessment: Professional Communication Scenarios

Learners will complete scenario-based assessments and case studies evaluating their ability to communicate effectively in professional settings, resolve conflicts, and demonstrate ethical conduct. The assessment includes written communication exercises, verbal communication role-plays, and analysis of professional ethics scenarios.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Communicate clearly and professionally in written and verbal formats
  2. Adapt communication style for technical and non-technical audiences
  3. Practice active listening and comprehension in conversations
  4. Collaborate effectively in team environments
  5. Resolve conflicts professionally and constructively
  6. Maintain professional conduct and ethical standards
  7. Support colleagues through mentoring and teamwork
