Learning Outcomes

  1. Identify professional writing tasks in a software development workplace where AI can reduce time and effort.
  2. Construct a structured AI prompt using role, task, context, and constraints to produce a first-draft workplace document.
  3. Apply a three-step review checklist — factual accuracy, appropriate tone, completeness — to an AI-generated draft.
  4. Write follow-up prompts to close specific gaps identified during draft review.
  5. Describe the key differences between communicating with engineering peers, product managers, and business stakeholders in a software development context.
  6. Extend an AI prompt with an audience brief that adjusts vocabulary, detail level, and tone for a target reader.
  7. Use AI to produce multiple audience-appropriate versions of the same content from a single source brief.
  8. Evaluate audience-adjusted AI output against a four-point rubric: vocabulary match, detail level, tone/formality, and actionability.
