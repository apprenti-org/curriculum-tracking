Syllabus: OSS Network Curriculum

Operations Support Specialist — Network 628 hours DRAFT

Course Description

The Operations Support Specialist — Network curriculum is a comprehensive apprenticeship program designed to prepare learners for roles managing and maintaining IT infrastructure and operations. This 628-hour Related Technical Instruction (RTI) curriculum, combined with 2,000 hours of on-the-job training (OJT), develops expertise in systems administration, cloud operations, automation, incident management, security, and professional skills. The curriculum aligns with DOL Registered Apprenticeship standards and prepares learners for industry certifications including CompTIA A+, CompTIA Network+, ITIL Foundation, and beyond.

Summative Assessment: Comprehensive Professional Competency Evaluation

Learners completing the full curriculum will demonstrate mastery across four key competency areas through a comprehensive portfolio evaluation that includes successful completion of individual course assessments, completion of OJT milestones, and demonstration of integrated competencies in a capstone project. The curriculum prepares learners for industry certifications and professional advancement in operations and infrastructure roles.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Administer and troubleshoot Linux and Windows systems in production environments
  2. Design and maintain network infrastructure with routing, switching, and security controls
  3. Monitor system performance and respond to incidents following ITIL practices
  4. Develop automation scripts and Infrastructure as Code solutions for repeatable deployments
  5. Implement CI/CD pipelines and continuous deployment strategies
  6. Apply security best practices and compliance frameworks to infrastructure
  7. Leverage artificial intelligence and data analysis for operational insights
  8. Communicate technically with diverse stakeholders and document complex systems
  9. Participate in organizational change management and continuous improvement
  10. Meet operational SLAs and service level objectives through proactive management

Labs & Practical Exercises

  • System administration labs across Linux and Windows platforms
  • Network design and troubleshooting scenarios
  • Incident management simulations
  • Infrastructure automation and CI/CD pipeline implementation
  • Security compliance assessments
  • Technical documentation and reporting exercises
  • Integrated capstone projects demonstrating competency across multiple areas
