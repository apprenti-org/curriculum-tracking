Syllabus: ITIL Specialist 4

Operations Support Specialist — Network 16 hours DRAFT

Course Description

ITIL Specialist 4 builds on foundational ITIL knowledge to develop advanced competencies in service management. This course explores the ITIL service value system in depth, covers sophisticated practices for service design and operations, and develops organizational leadership capabilities. Learners will understand how to implement enterprise-grade ITIL practices, manage complex operational challenges, drive continuous improvement at scale, and achieve ITIL Specialist certification. The course is designed for professionals ready to take on strategic service management roles.

Summative Assessment: Strategic Service Management Project

Learners will develop a comprehensive service management strategy for a complex organizational scenario. The assessment evaluates advanced understanding of ITIL concepts, ability to design integrated service value systems, and capacity to lead organizational change. Learners will present their strategy, addressing governance, operations, improvement, and stakeholder management. The assessment also prepares learners for the ITIL Specialist certification exam.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Apply service value chain principles to design complex service ecosystems
  2. Implement organizational governance frameworks aligned with ITIL practices
  3. Design and optimize service portfolios across multiple value streams
  4. Manage enterprise-scale changes and transformation initiatives
  5. Lead complex incident investigation and root cause analysis
  6. Implement proactive problem management and risk mitigation
  7. Develop business continuity and disaster recovery strategies
  8. Drive organizational change management and adoption
  9. Establish advanced metrics and analytics for service improvement
  10. Demonstrate leadership and strategic thinking in service management
